Problem / Data Domain

Music has a powerful link to human emotion, yet most music platforms focus only on recommendation algorithms, not on how users feel. College students often turn to songs to regulate or express moods, but there‚Äôs no simple, safe, and creative space to document and share those emotional connections.

Existing apps like Spotify or Moodify focus on large-scale playlists or AI-generated mixes, but they rarely let users actively reflect on their emotions. Our app seeks to fill this gap by enabling users to connect mood, music, and memory in one personal, interactive platform.

Related apps: MoodPlaylist, Spotify

App Description

Music with Mood is an interactive emotional playlist app that allows users to create mood-based song cards, organize playlists, and share them with others. The goal is to help users reflect on emotions, discover music from the community, and build connections through shared feelings.

Target Users

Primary users are college students and young adults who use music to express or regulate emotions. The app will also consider accessibility for multilingual users and inclusive design for emotional diversity.
